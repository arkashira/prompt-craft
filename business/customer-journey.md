 # Customer-Journey.md

## Aware

### Trigger Event
- Discovery of a specific pain point in developing and maintaining high-quality Language Model (LLM) prompts.
- Awareness of the need for a tool to improve the effectiveness and efficiency of LLM prompts.

### Friction Points
- Lack of knowledge about the existence of a solution for the specific pain point.
- Difficulty in finding a tool that caters to the unique needs of their specific use case.

### User Emotions
- Frustration due to the time and resources wasted on ineffective LLM prompts.
- Anxiety about the potential impact on their project's success.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Providing educational content about the benefits of using a specialized tool for LLM prompt development.
- Offering a free trial or demo to showcase the tool's capabilities.

### Metric
- Awareness rate (number of users who become aware of Prompt-Craft).

## Consider

### Trigger Event
- Researching potential solutions for the pain point.
- Comparing Prompt-Craft with other tools in the market.

### Friction Points
- Understanding the features and benefits of Prompt-Craft compared to other tools.
- Evaluating the cost-effectiveness of Prompt-Craft.

### User Emotions
- Skepticism about the tool's ability to deliver on its promises.
- Excitement about the potential improvements in their LLM prompt development process.

### Opportunities to Delight
- Providing detailed product demos and case studies.
- Offering a money-back guarantee to alleviate any risk.

### Metric
- Consideration rate (number of users who consider using Prompt-Craft).

## Try

### User Emotions
