---

**Strategic Notes**

- **Differentiation**: Unlike generic prompt‑generation tools, *prompt‑craft* offers a full end‑to‑end workflow: design → test → iterate → deploy, backed by quantitative analytics and cost controls.  
- **Monetization Leverage**: The prompt marketplace creates a network effect; community creators earn revenue while enriching the platform’s value.  
- **Scalability**: Leveraging existing Axentx infrastructure (shared BRAIN, data pipelines) reduces time‑to‑market for new features and ensures rapid self‑improvement loops.  
- **Risk Mitigation**: Early focus on compliance and security will be critical for enterprise adoption; plan for SOC2/ISO27001 audits within the first 12 months.
